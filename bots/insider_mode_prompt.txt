INSIDER MODE v4.1 — Operating Ruleset
======================================

This is the strategy profile MemeRunner AI runs under. It was originally
authored as standing instructions for a Base44 Superagent and is reproduced
here so the standalone GitHub Actions bot stays behaviorally identical.

ENTRY
-----
- Strict fresh-listing filter: only tokens under 8 minutes old (unless a
  momentum re-entry waiver applies — see below).
- Target liquidity sweet spot: $15,000–$120,000. Below this is too thin
  (wash-tradeable), above this the token has already run and isn't a fresh
  entry anymore.
- Reject any token with >35% single-holder concentration (pre-entry dev/
  insider bag check) unless that top holder is the liquidity pool itself.
- Entry gate: quantum score >= 0.55 for W9 (main sniper, more capital,
  higher risk tolerance), >= 0.70 for W8 (secondary, stricter).
- Momentum re-entry carve-out: a token we've bought before can bypass the
  8-minute freshness window if its 5-minute price change is still >= 15%.

EXIT — scalp-first tiered take-profit (v7.5)
---------------------------------------------
1. SCALP_TP at +15% unrealized gain → sell 45% of the remaining position.
   This is the most important behavioral change: bank real profit early
   instead of waiting for +20% and risking a full round-trip back to
   breakeven or a loss on a reversal.
2. TIER1_TP at +20% → sell 40% of whatever remains after the scalp.
3. TIER2_TP at +60% → sell 30% of whatever remains after tier 1.
4. The remainder rides a trailing stop (arms at +10%, exits on an 8%
   pullback from peak) or the hard stop-loss / max-hold safety net below.

RISK MANAGEMENT
----------------
- Hard stop-loss: -8%, non-negotiable.
- Max hold time: 15 minutes. If the price feed goes stale (dead API) past
  this point, force-exit using the REAL on-chain token balance rather than
  waiting indefinitely.
- All exit/entry accounting requires genuine on-chain confirmation
  (txHash + confirmed status) — an accepted-but-unconfirmed transaction is
  never counted as a completed trade.

PROFIT LOCKING
--------------
- Any realized profitable exit with pnl_sol >= 0.01 sweeps 50% of that gain
  to the vault wallet (W1, non-trading, idle by design) via a real on-chain
  SOL transfer. The other 50% + principal stays in the trading wallet to
  keep compounding.

CAPITAL STRUCTURE
-----------------
- 2 concentrated trading wallets: W9 (main sniper, most capital, looser
  gate) and W8 (secondary, stricter gate). This replaced an earlier
  fragmented 10-wallet approach that diluted capital too thin to matter
  per trade.
